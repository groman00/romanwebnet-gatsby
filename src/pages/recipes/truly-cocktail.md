## Ingredients
* 1 Glass of Ice
* 1 shot Vodka or Gin
* Splash of Lime Juice (or Cranberry)
* 1 Can of Truly (any flavour)

## Directions

### Step 1
Glass of ice.  Shot vodka or gin.   A splash of lime juice (or cranberry).  Fill the rest with Truli.
